Analysis of Artifact

However, today’s social networks are more than tools to promote communications between people all around the world. Consider the applications of social networking that can be operated on various portable devices, such as smartphones and tablets. Their capability of disseminating information far exceeds that of newspapers, television, or even websites. One significant example of this capability is Twitter’s effects on stock market. As a tool allowing instant sharing of information, Twitter is able to affect investors’ decisions significantly. On August 13, 2013 Apple’s stock price surged by 5 percentage -gaining about 17 billion in market value – after billionaire Car Icahn made an announcement related to Apple on Twitter. Although it cannot be proved to be a causal effect, it is undeniable that the two events are correlated. Statisticians at Indiana University, School of Informatics and Computing, published a research paper – Twitter mood predicts the stock market – and gave a result demonstrating a correlation between Tweeter comments and stock market movement. Behavioral economics, individual are not rational as a consumer. Decisions made by individuals are always affected by cognitive biases, no matter how smart the individuals may be.
