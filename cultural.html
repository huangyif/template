Executive Summary

People’s life changes significantly with the widespread use of Internet. As a new way of communication, social networking almost maximizes the impact of Internet on the society. In China, social networking has its unique impact on the country. Since the Internet is less regulated than other media in China, social networking provides more freedom of speech for Chinese citizens. However, in some cases, social networking is also be a place of wrongdoings with less law enforcement applied. This essay analyze social networking’s both positive and negative impacts on the nation and then suggests solutions for potential problems.
